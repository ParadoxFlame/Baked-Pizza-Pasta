Preheat oven to broil.

Heat olive oil in a large oven-proof skillet over medium high heat. Add meat toppingof choice and cook until browned, about 3-5 minutes, drain excess fat. 
Stir in 1/4 cup olives until heated through, about 1 minute.
Stir in tomato sauce, oregano, basil garlic powder, pasta and 1 1/2 cups water; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat and simmer until pasta is cooked through, about 12-14 minutes.
Remove from heat; top with mozzarella and remaining 1/4 cup oolives. Place into oven and cook until melted and golden brown, about 2 minutes.
Serve immediately, garnished with parsley, if desired.
